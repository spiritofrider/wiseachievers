Most Relevant FAQs

What is a Career?

Life is a journey. Every individual wishes to and has a right to make this journey a happy one. One of the major areas which decide how happy you will be in life is the area of the occupation which you choose to work in. In other words your ‘Career’ or even stated as one’s calling in life. It is what you would do mostly on a daily basis in order to earn your money and also attain other goals and objectives


What is Career Assessment ?

It is a well known and now a psychologically proven fact that nobody can be a master of all things and that nobody is such that he or she cannot do anything. We may have the knowledge and ability to do many things but can master mostly one and it’s related things. Finding out what is that one thing or its related things that the person is or would be good at and should take it up as his or her occupation is known as Career Assessment.


What is Career Guidance and Counseling?

Once Career Assessment is done accurately there are still many challenges faced, academically and otherwise on the road to achieving this chosen field as your work area or profession. Career guidance and Counseling is the process whereby experts in the field of guidance or even in the field chosen by the individual guide and counsel this individual to attain high levels of proficiency.
